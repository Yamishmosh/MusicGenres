ELECTRONIC MUSIC



What is Electronic Music?

Electronic music is music that employs electronic musical instruments, digital instruments and circuitry-based music technology. In general, a distinction can be made between sound produced using electromechanical means (electroacoustic music), and that produced using electronics only.


SUBCATEGORIES

Chiptune
Chiptune, also known as chip music, is a style of synthesized electronic music made using the programmable sound generator sound chips or synthesizers in vintage arcade machines, computers and video game consoles.
  • Anamanaguchi
  • Sabrepulse
  • She
Industrial
Industrial music is a genre of music that draws on harsh, transgressive or provocative sounds and themes.
  • Skinny Puppy
  • Nine Inch Nails
  • Ministry
Drum and Bass
Drum and bass is a genre of electronic music characterised by fast breakbeats with heavy bass and sub-bass lines, sampled sources, and synthesizers.
  • Noisia
  • Andy C
  • Pendulum
