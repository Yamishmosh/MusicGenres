ALTERNATIVE MUSIC



What is Alternative Music?

It is a less intense version of rock and a more intense version of pop. Typically strays from the generic lyrics of pop.


SUBCATEGORIES

Alternative Rock
Alternative rock is a term used to categorize rock music that emerged from the independent music underground of the 1970s and became widely popular in the 1980s.
  • Foster the People
  • Thirty Seconds to Mars
  • Blur
Lo-fi
Lo-fi is a music or production quality in which elements usually regarded as imperfections of a recording or performance are audible, sometimes as a deliberate aesthetic choice.
  • Jinsang
  • Nymano
  • Ninjoi
Goth Rock
Gothic rock is a style of rock music that emerged from post-punk in the late 1970s.
  • Bauhaus
  • The Sisters of Mercy
  • The Cure
