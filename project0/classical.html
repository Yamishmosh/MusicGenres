CLASSICAL MUSIC



What is Classical Music?

Classical music is art music produced or rooted in the traditions of Western culture, including both liturgical and secular music.


SUBCATEGORIES

Contemporary Classical
Contemporary classical music is classical music relative to the present day. At the beginning of the 21st century, it commonly referred to the post-1945 modern forms of post-tonal music.
  • Steve Reich
  • Philip Glass
  • John Cage
Medieval
Medieval music consists of songs, instrumental pieces, and liturgical music from about 500 A.D. to 1400.
  • Perotin
  • Corvus Corax
  • Hildegard of Bingen
Opera
Opera is a form of theatre in which music has a leading role and the parts are taken by singers, but is distinct from musical theatre.
  • Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart
  • Giacomo Puccini
  • Luciano Pavarotti
